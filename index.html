Empowering Youth, Shaping Nepal's Future

We are a non-profit organization dedicated to inspiring, educating, and empowering the youth of Nepal for a brighter tomorrow.

About Us

President: Dipendra Claulaigai
Message: "Together, we can build a future where every young Nepali has the opportunity to thrive and lead."

Youth for Future Nepal (YFF Nepal) is committed to fostering leadership, education, and social responsibility among the youth of Nepal. We believe in the power of young minds to create positive change in their communities and beyond.

Our Projects

Youth Leadership Workshops
Training the next generation of leaders through interactive workshops and mentorship programs.
Community Clean-Up Drives
Promoting environmental awareness and action by organizing clean-up events in local communities.
Education for All
Supporting underprivileged children with access to quality education and school supplies.
Health & Wellness Camps
Focusing on physical and mental well-being of youth through health camps and awareness sessions.
